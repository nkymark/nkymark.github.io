---
title:  "Research Showcased at the *ITF 2025 Summit*"
date:   2025-05-23 09:00:00 +0000
---

Our work on "Digital Twin of a Turbocharged Spark-Ignited Engine System for Control and Fault Diagnosis" was showcased by the Department for Transport (DfT) UK at the *International Transport Forum (ITF) 2025 Summit on Transport Resilience to Global Shocks*, which was held 21--23 May in Leipzig, Germany, as part of current highlights of UK academic research related to transport digital twinning.
