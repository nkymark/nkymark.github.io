The [MAvRiC](https://www.markusng.com/mavric) will be featured and showcased in the upcoming Northern Ireland Science Festival. 

"Robots at Work and Play" will be a full-day event held in Belfast, and there will be loads of fun activities for the young and grown-up alike. 

Discover how robots are revolutionising both work and play!

Visitors can try their hands at precision control of robotic arms to perform delicate tasks, and witness 3D printers creating products from everyday items to industrial components.

There will also be a fun workshop for children to build their own miniature robots!
