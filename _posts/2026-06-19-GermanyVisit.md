---
title: "Visit to the Universities of Applied Sciences in Augsburg and Kempten"
date: 2026-06-19 09:00:00 +0000
---

Mark Ng recently visited the Technical University of Applied Sciences Augsburg in Germany with Dr Robert McMurray from the School of Engineering, Ulster University. The primary goal of the visit was to strengthen the exchange collaboration between Ulster and Augsburg. As part of this effort, we interviewed several students who will be coming for an exchange in Belfast next year. Additionally, we served as examiners for some of their Final Year Project presentations.

Following the visit to Augsburg, we traveled to the Kempten University of Applied Sciences. During this stay, Mark Ng had the opportunity to give a talk on Digital Twins and promote the MAvRiC robotics lab at Ulster.
