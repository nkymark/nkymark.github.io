---
title:  "Robots at Work and Play – NI Science Festival 2026"
date:   2026-02-21 09:00:00 +0000
---

"Robots at Work and Play" was a resounding success! The [MAvRiC](https://www.markusng.com/mavric) team hosted a full-day event at the LG Foyer, Ulster University Belfast Campus, as part of the Northern Ireland Science Festival on Saturday, 21st February 2026.

This academic-led and student-run event welcomed around 200 visitors across the five-hour session, drawing in families, students, and industry professionals alike. The event showcased the breadth of robotics research and technology available at MAvRiC, with hands-on activities that engaged visitors of all ages.

Highlights of the day included:
* Live demonstrations of **collaborative robotic arms (cobots)**, where visitors could witness and interact with precision robot manipulation up close
* A quadruped robot demonstration that drew large and enthusiastic crowds
* **3D printing showcases**, with Bambulab printers creating objects from everyday items through to industrial components in real time
* A **miniature robot-building workshop** for children, proving a huge hit with younger visitors

The event also featured an industry showcase from **Elite Electronic Systems Limited**, who demonstrated non-destructive testing (NDT) technologies alongside the MAvRiC team, highlighting the real-world applications of the lab's research across aerospace, manufacturing, and materials development.

A big thank you to all the staff and students who helped make the event such a success, and to everyone who came along on the day!
