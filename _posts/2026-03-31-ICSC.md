---
title:  "Invited Speaker at the Irish Control Systems Colloquium 2026"
date:   2026-03-31 09:00:00 +0000
---

Mark Ng has been invited as a speaker at the [Irish Control Systems Colloquium 2026](https://www.mishe.ie/control-systems/), an IFACx event hosted by ATU Sligo on behalf of the Irish Systems and Control Committee. The colloquium brings together researchers from across Ireland to showcase current advances in systems and control engineering and to promote collaboration in the field.

The technical programme takes place on Friday 24th April 2026 at ATU Sligo. Mark will deliver a talk entitled:
**"Digital Twins, Fault Diagnosis, and Simulation Testbeds: From Automotive Engines to Advanced Manufacturing"**

Other confirmed speakers include Dr. Pantelis Sopasakis (QUB), Dr. Marion McAfee (ATU), Dr. Edin Omerdic (UL), Dr. Aidan O'Dwyer (TU Dublin), Prof. John Ringwood (Maynooth University), Patrick Lawless (ABB), Dr. Panpan Zhou (University of Galway), Dr. Siyuan Zhan (TCD), and Prof. Vikram Pakrashi (UCD).
