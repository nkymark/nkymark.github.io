---
title: "KTP with Elite Electronic Systems Awarded 'Outstanding' Grade by Innovate UK"
date: 2026-07-21 09:00:00 +0000
---

Our KTP project with Elite Electronic Systems Ltd has been awarded the highest grade of **'Outstanding'** by the Innovate UK's independent assessment panel!

This 2-year partnership with Elite set out to embed advanced digital manufacturing capabilities within the company's Surface Mount Technology (SMT) process, developing a digital twin (DT) to enhance fault detection and isolation, as well as real-time production monitoring for PCB assembly.

Towards the end of this project, we have achieved the following:
- A real-time Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) data pipeline with SQL and Power BI dashboards on the shop floor
- A DT-based AOI threshold optimisation model to reduce false inspection flags
- A ML-based predictive maintenance system for SMT turrets
- An OCR-based solution to automate conversion of customer BoMs into production-ready formats

The project also delivered a peer-reviewed conference paper at the IEEE INDIN 2025, as well as other poster presentations and public engagements, including showcasing the work to 200+ visitors at the NI Science Festival 2026's "Robots at Work and Play" event.
